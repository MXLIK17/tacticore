# TactiCore

TactiCore is a football draft and simulation web application that allows users to build custom squads using historical football players and compete through simulated competitions.

Players can create their dream XI by drafting from legendary Premier League clubs and FIFA World Cup national teams, choose formations, and test their squad through realistic football simulations.

The goal of TactiCore is to combine football history, tactical decision-making, and simulation into a simple and engaging experience.

---

## Features

### Squad Drafting
- Build custom teams using historical football players.
- Draft players based on position and tactical requirements.
- Create squads from legendary Premier League clubs and World Cup teams.

### Tactical Squad Builder
- Interactive football pitch interface.
- Formation selection and position-based squad building.
- Visual representation of drafted players and team structure.

### Competition Modes

**Premier League Mode**
- Simulate a complete 38-match league season.
- Compete against historical teams.
- Track season results and player performances.

**World Cup Mode**
- Compete through a full international tournament.
- Group stage and knockout rounds.
- Attempt to become World Champions.

### Match Simulation
- Custom football simulation engine.
- Generates match outcomes, goals, and player statistics.
- Maintains competition rules and progression.

## Development Goals

TactiCore focuses on creating a simple and accessible football experience that emphasizes:

- Tactical squad building
- Football history
- Simulation systems
- Clean user experience

Rather than traditional football management complexity, TactiCore focuses on the excitement of building a dream team and seeing how it performs against football history.
